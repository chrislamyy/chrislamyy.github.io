Bluebird Mauritius

Blue Bird is an enchanting little resort in Pereybere, in the north of the island of Mauritius. For over twenty years, we have been welcoming guests from all over the world. Many fall in love with our island and come back again.

Located in Pereybere with its beautiful sandy beach, shops and restaurants is within a few minutes walk. At the public beach you can practice your favorite water sport, rent deckchairs and umbrellas or just relax and admire the stunning colors of the sea. Sanitary facilities, are available.

On weekends the public beach gets crowded due to its popularity amongst local Mauritians, whereby you can experience a very different atmosphere.

Gorgeous beach walks heading north towards Cap Malheureux are possible. On the way you will find many secluded coves that invite you to relax and swim.
